How can we help?

Quick answers for the most common questions.

iCloud sync

Sign in to the same Apple Account on each device and enable iCloud Drive for Cocktail Guide Trainer. Keep the app open briefly after making changes so CloudKit can finish syncing.

Menu scanning

Use a bright, straight-on photo with readable text. Review every detected drink before saving. You can edit a result or cancel the scan whenever the menu is interpreted incorrectly.

PIN & Face ID

If you forget your four-digit PIN, use the Face ID or Touch ID recovery option on the lock screen. Biometric authentication happens through Apple system security.

Custom glassware

Open the Glassware Library and choose Add Glassware. You can save a custom name, serving notes, pairings, and a photo for the vessels you use.

Cocktail photos

Use the photo tools to keep the full drink in frame. On supported images, background removal can isolate the cocktail and glassware for a clean presentation.

Privacy & data

The app has no ads, tracking, or third-party cloud AI. Your library stays on your devices and in your private iCloud when sync is enabled.
